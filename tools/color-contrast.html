WCAG Contrast Standards

AA Normal — 4.5:1 minimum for text under 18px. AA Large — 3:1 for text 18px+ or bold 14px+. AAA Normal — 7:1 for enhanced accessibility. Proper contrast ensures your content is readable by users with visual impairments.
