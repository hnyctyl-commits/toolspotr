What Makes a Password Strong?

Three factors determine password strength: length (longer is better), complexity (mix of uppercase, lowercase, digits, symbols), and unpredictability (avoid common patterns, dictionary words, personal info). A 16-char password with all character types has more entropy than most people realize.
