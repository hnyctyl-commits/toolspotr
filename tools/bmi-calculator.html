Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measure based on weight and height. Categories: Underweight (<18.5), Normal (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), Obese Class I (30-34.9), Class II (35-39.9), Class III (≥40). While BMI is a useful screening tool, it doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition.
