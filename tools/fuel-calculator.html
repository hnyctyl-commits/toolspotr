Fuel Costs

Knowing your trip's fuel cost helps with road trip budgeting. Average US fuel economy is about 25 MPG. Gas prices vary by state — California averages $4.50+, Texas around $3.00. This tool uses simple math: (distance ÷ MPG) × price per gallon = total cost.
