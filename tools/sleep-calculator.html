Sleep Cycles

The average sleep cycle lasts about 90 minutes. Waking up in the middle of a cycle leaves you feeling groggy (sleep inertia). This calculator helps you wake up at the end of a cycle — feeling refreshed. Adults need 4-6 cycles (6-9 hours) per night.
