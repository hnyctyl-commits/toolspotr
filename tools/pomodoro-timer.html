Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. Work in focused 25-minute intervals, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break. This method improves focus, reduces mental fatigue, and helps you get more done.
