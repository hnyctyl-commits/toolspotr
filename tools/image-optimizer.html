Why Optimize Images?

Images account for over 50% of a typical webpage's size. Optimized images load faster, improve SEO rankings, reduce bandwidth costs, and provide a better user experience. A well-optimized WebP image can be 25-35% smaller than JPEG at the same quality. All processing happens in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
