Pregnancy Due Date

The average pregnancy lasts 40 weeks (280 days) from the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP). Only about 4% of babies arrive on their exact due date — it's an estimate. Conception typically occurs about 2 weeks after LMP. The three trimesters are approximately 1-13, 14-27, and 28-40 weeks.
