What is Browser Fingerprinting?

Browser fingerprinting is a tracking technique that collects unique characteristics of your browser and device — screen resolution, installed fonts, timezone, language, plugins, and more. Combined, these can create a nearly unique identifier (fingerprint) that follows you across the web, even without cookies. This tool shows you what information your browser exposes.
