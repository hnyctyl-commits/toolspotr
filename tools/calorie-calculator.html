Calorie Basics

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the calories your body needs at rest. TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) includes activity. For weight loss, subtract 500 calories/day (~0.5kg/week). For weight gain, add 500 calories/day. These are estimates — individual results vary.

❓ Calorie Calculator FAQ

How many calories should I eat to lose weight?

Subtract 300-500 calories from your TDEE for safe weight loss of about 0.5kg (1lb) per week. Never go below your BMR. For most people, 1,500-1,800 calories/day for women and 2,000-2,500 for men is a common weight loss range.
