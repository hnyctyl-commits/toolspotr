📊 Barcode Generator

Generate EAN-13 barcodes from any 12-digit number. Download as PNG for product labels, inventory tracking, and more.

EAN-13 Barcodes

EAN-13 is the standard barcode format used worldwide for retail products. It encodes 13 digits — 12 data digits plus a check digit. The first digits indicate the country (e.g., 590 = Poland, 690-699 = China, 000-019 = US/Canada). This tool generates barcodes you can use for product labels and inventory.
