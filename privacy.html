🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Our Commitment

Toolflow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website at toolspotr.com.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All tools on Toolflow run entirely in your browser. No text, images, files, or other content you interact with is uploaded to any server.

The only data processed is:

  • Anonymous usage analytics via Google Analytics (page views, browser type, approximate location — no personal identifiers)
  • Local storage — some tools save preferences in your browser's localStorage (e.g., theme preference, notepad content). This data never leaves your device.

Cookies

Toolflow uses minimal cookies:

  • Google Analytics — to understand how visitors use the site (anonymous)
  • Local storage — for remembering your theme preference and language

No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no third-party marketing cookies are used.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-Party Services

Toolflow uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising (future)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store user data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to personal information from our end. All tool processing happens locally in your browser.

Children's Privacy

Toolflow does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All tools are general-purpose and do not require registration or personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
