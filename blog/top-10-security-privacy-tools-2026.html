1. 🛡️ DNS Leak Test

You use a VPN to protect your privacy. But is it actually working? A DNS leak test reveals whether your DNS queries are going through your VPN tunnel or leaking to your ISP. If your DNS server location differs from your VPN location, your browsing data is exposed — even with a VPN active.

This tool compares your visible IP address against your DNS server IP. If they match, you're protected. If they differ, your ISP can see every website you visit.

💡 Tip: Run this test after connecting to any new VPN server. A quick 10-second check can save your privacy.

3. 🔑 Password Generator

Weak passwords are responsible for over 80% of data breaches. A strong password generator creates random passwords that are mathematically impossible to crack. The best password is long (16+ characters), uses all character types, and contains no predictable patterns.

This tool uses the browser's crypto.getRandomValues() API — the same cryptographic standard used by banks — to generate truly random passwords.

💡 Tip: A 16-character password with mixed case, numbers, and symbols would take a supercomputer billions of years to crack. Use a password manager to store them.
